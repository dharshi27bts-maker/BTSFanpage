J Hope

Full Name: Jung Hoseok

Position: main dancer, rapper

Birthdate: February 18, 1994

There’s a good reason J-Hope is often referred to as “Our Hope” — his energy can light up a stadium. But if you look closer, J-Hope’s greatest artistic achievement isn’t just being a “sunshine” figure or the “hope” of his band, BTS; it’s his bravery in showing us how heavy that sun can be.
