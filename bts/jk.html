Jungkook

Full Name: Jeon Jungkook

Position: main dancer, lead vocalist

Birthdate: September 1, 1997

BTS member Jungkook rose to prominence as a member and vocalist of South Korean boy band BTS. He has performed three solo songs as part of BTS' discography— "Begin" in 2016, "Euphoria" in 2018, and "My Time" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart.
